    Operator Notes

    This interactive homage reimagines the 1992 SimRefinery console with a three.js map and retro controls. Adjust levers, explore process flow, and watch how refinery choices ripple across the plant while dashboards grade your performance.

    • Click any process unit to inspect throughput, utilization, and incident history.
    • Shift-drag or use the right mouse button to pan the map; scroll the mouse wheel to zoom.
    • Monitor the hovering gauges and alerts—yellow indicates strain, red marks critical incidents.
    • The Operations Grade panel blends profit, reliability, and emissions into a running score.
